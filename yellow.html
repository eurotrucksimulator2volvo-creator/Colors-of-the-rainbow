Yellow

Yellow is the third color of the rainbow.

Fun facts about yellow and its wavelength

  1. Yellow is associated with happiness, optimism, and sunshine.
  2. Yellow is often used to grab attention and create a sense of caution.
  3. Yellow is a primary color in the RYB color model.
  4. Yellow is a secondary color in the RGB color model, created by mixing green and red.
  5. Yellow is commonly used in warning signs and traffic signals for high visibility, alongside orange.
