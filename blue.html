Blue

Blue is the fifth color of the rainbow.

Fun facts about blue and its wavelength

  1. Blue's wavelength is less energetic than green's.
  2. Blue is associated with trust, loyalty, and stability.
  3. Blue is often used to represent calmness and serenity.
  4. Blue is a primary color in the RYB color model.
  5. Blue is a primary color in the RGB color model.
  6. Blue is commonly used in branding and marketing to convey reliability and professionalism.
