Red

Red is the first color of the rainbow.

Fun facts about red and its wavelength

  1. Red has the longest wavelength of all visible colors.
  2. Red is associated with love and passion.
  3. Red is often used to grab attention and signal danger.
  4. Red is a primary color in the RGB color model.
  5. At night, red light is less disruptive to night vision and sleep.
