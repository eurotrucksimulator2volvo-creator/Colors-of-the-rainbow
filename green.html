Green

Green is the fourth color of the rainbow.

Fun facts about green and its wavelength

  1. Green's wavelength is more energetic than yellow's.
  2. Green is associated with nature, growth, and harmony.
  3. Green is often used to represent safety and permission.
  4. Green is a secondary color in the RYB color model, created by mixing blue and yellow.
  5. Green is a primary color in the RGB color model, created by mixing blue and red.
  6. Green is commonly used in traffic lights to indicate "go" and in environmental campaigns to symbolize eco-friendliness.
