Violet

Violet is the seventh color of the rainbow.

Fun facts about violet and its wavelength

  1. Violet has the shortest wavelength of all visible colors, and has the highest energy.
  2. Violet is associated with royalty, luxury, and spirituality.
  3. Violet is often used to create a sense of mystery and intrigue.
  4. Violet is a secondary color in the RYB color model, created by mixing red and blue.
  5. Violet is a tertiary color in the RGB color model, created by mixing blue and magenta.
  6. Violet is commonly used in art and design to evoke creativity and imagination.
