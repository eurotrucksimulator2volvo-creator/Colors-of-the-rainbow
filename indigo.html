Indigo

Indigo is the sixth color of the rainbow.

Fun facts about indigo and its wavelength

  1. Indigo's wavelength is less energetic than violet's.
  2. Indigo is associated with intuition, insight, and wisdom.
  3. Indigo is often used to represent creativity and imagination.
  4. Indigo is commonly used in fashion and interior design to create a sense of luxury and sophistication.
  5. People sometimes confuse indigo with blue or purple. In reality, it's a combination of both.
