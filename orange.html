Orange

Orange is the second color of the rainbow.

Fun facts about orange and its wavelength

  1. Orange's wavelength is more energetic than red's.
  2. Orange is associated with energy, warmth, and enthusiasm.
  3. Orange is often used to draw attention and create a sense of urgency.
  4. Orange is a secondary color in the RGB color model, created by mixing red and yellow.
  5. Orange is commonly used in safety equipment and traffic signs for high visibility, alongside yellow.
  6. Orange is the only color in the rainbow that shares its name with a fruit.
